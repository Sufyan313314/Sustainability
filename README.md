## EcoVadis Consultancy

I work as a sustainability consultant helping companies achieve the best possible EcoVadis scores. EcoVadis is the world's leading sustainability ratings platform — used by over 130,000 companies globally to assess supplier sustainability performance.

**What I do for clients:**

- Conduct a full gap analysis of the client's current sustainability position against EcoVadis scoring criteria
- Identify weaknesses across the four EcoVadis themes: Environment, Labour & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement
- Develop and deliver corrective action plans tailored to each company's industry and size
- Create and submit supporting evidence documentation on behalf of clients
- Provide ongoing advisory to maintain and improve scores year-on-year

**Industries served:**

## EV Charger Business

**[evchargers.com.pk](https://evchargers.com.pk)** — Pakistan's first dedicated e-commerce platform for EV chargers.

Co-founded with my brother while finishing school, this business has grown from a regional distributor into a fully operational company with its own branded product line.

**Key milestones:**

- Started as the official distributor of **Rolec** (UK) in Pakistan — one of the world's leading EV charging brands with 30+ years of experience
- Expanded to stock **Wallbox** (Spain) and **Solinteg** (China) alongside Rolec
- Launched our own brand — **RNR Smart EV Charger** — manufactured in China, covering 7.4 kW to 22 kW residential and commercial solutions
- Built the company website fully in-house using WordPress
- Delivered residential installations (7 kW–22 kW) and commercial charging solutions (30 kW–60 kW)
- Clients include businesses, parking operators, and EV car brand (BYD, Audi) project installations

**Products offered:**

- Residential chargers: 7.2 kW – 22 kW (home charging)
- Commercial chargers: 30 kW – 60 kW (parking, fleet, business)
- DC fast chargers: up to 120 kW (pump stations)
- Full range of accessories: cables, adapters, RFID & billing systems

## GHG Emissions Calculations

I calculate greenhouse gas emissions for clients as part of their EcoVadis environmental reporting and wider ESG disclosure requirements.

**Methodology:**

- **Scope 1** — Direct emissions from company-owned sources (fuel combustion, company vehicles)
- **Scope 3** — Indirect value chain emissions (business travel, purchased goods, waste, freight)
- Calculations use the **UK Government GHG Conversion Factors** (published annually by DESNZ / DEFRA)
- Outputs delivered as structured Excel workbooks with clear assumptions, data inputs, and emission totals by category

> Example GHG calculation workbooks (with dummy data) are included in this repository.
